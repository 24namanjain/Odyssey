Benefits:

1. Flexible

1. Removes if else branches

1. Easy to add new strategies

Disadvantages:

1. More complex code

1. More classes to maintain

1. More dependencies

When to use:

1. When you have a lot of different strategies

1. When you need to add new strategies easily

1. When you need to remove strategies easily

1. When you need to change the strategy easily

1. When you need to test the strategy easily
